Workshops

About

Partnering with Science Oxford, this year we will be providing introductory workshops to 3D printing, 3D modelling, electronics and robotics. We welcome people from all background and experience levels to join us. Workshops are designed for beginners with absolutely no experience at all.

Project workshops are your opportunity to work on your own individual project, or join in with other society members.

Project Workshop

Teaching workshops start at 13:00. For those doing individual or group projects, simply pop-in anytime between 13:00 and 17:00. All our workshop activities will take place at Make Oxford in the Oxford Centre for Innovation.

Workshop Managers

If you have a project that we might be interested, and you would like to be the person leading it, please contact our workshop managers.

We are interested in projects of the following categories:

  • 3D Printing, Modelling and Design Projects
  • 3D Arts and Fashion Projects
  • Robotics and Mechanics Projects
  • IOT Projects
