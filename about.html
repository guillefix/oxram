About Us

The Society

The Oxford 3D Printing society (Ox3DP) is the first University Society in the UK for 3D printing and additive manufacturing. Embedded in the University of Oxford, we are an educational and research platform that aims to empower both academia and industry to impact the upcoming industrial revolution in the most positive way.

Projects & Activities

Education

  • Talks & tours
  • Hackspace
  • Social media

Networking

  • Connecting academia and industry

Consulting and Research

  • Short term - optimal exploitation of the state-of-the-art additive manufacturing technologies
  • Long term - the future of design and manufacturing
