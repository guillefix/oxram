Definition

Additive Manufacturing (AM)

Video credit: University of Sheffield

A process of joining materials to make objects from 3D model data, usually layer upon layer, as opposed to subtractive methodologies.

 

 

3D Printing

Video credit: Global Television 16x9

A term often used synonymously with additive manufacturing; in particular associated with low end or the consumer industry.
